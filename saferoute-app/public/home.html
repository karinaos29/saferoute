The Challenge

Frameworks exist.
Execution fails.

Candidates face a maze of fragmented pathways. When existing legal structures fail to connect the dots, evidence is lost and cases go unacted. SafeRoute closes the gap between incident and institutional response.

The Problem

Fractured Infrastructure

Current frameworks struggle to handle the modern scale of online abuse directed at political candidates.

  • Candidates don't know the proper reporting channels
  • Evidence is discarded without structure
  • Institutions receive scattered, unactionable screenshots
The Solution

Structured Intelligence

SafeRoute steps up where processes break down, preserving evidence and protecting targeted candidates.

  • AI identifies and categorises the exact abuse type
  • Evidence is preserved, time-stamped, and packaged
  • Institutions track cases via secure login
Core Innovation

The Referral Intelligence Engine

Most reporting tools stop after simply collecting a report. SafeRoute actively evaluates the submission, categorises the legal framework required, determines urgency, and prepares it for the correct institutional body—automatically, proportionately, and with a human in the loop at every critical gate before any referral is made.

The Pipeline

Four Steps. One Route.

Every submission follows a single, defined pipeline—taking raw evidence and turning it into an institutional referral.

01

Evidence Submission

Candidates (or their teams) upload screenshots, URLs, or text. The intake safely captures target data, platform, and dates.

02

AI Categorisation

OCR extracts text. NLP models analyze the context, assess severity, and automatically identify the specific subcategory of hate.

03

Recommendation

The engine maps the identified abuse type to the proper legal framework and generates an escalation pathway for institutions.

04

Case Generation

A structured case file with timelines and evidence is securely exported to authorities for tracking and immediate action.

Human Rights

Safeguards built in,
not bolted on.

Technology must serve democratic values. We embed rights directly into the platform architecture to protect candidates and free expression alike.

01

Privacy-by-Design

Sensitive PII is encrypted at rest and in transit. Strict role-based access control guarantees zero public exposure of candidate data.

02

Explainable AI (XAI)

Every risk score features a plain-language rationale. Operators see exactly why cases are flagged, eliminating black-box decisions.

03

Mandatory Human Review

No automated escalations. A trained operator must validate every case file before it hits institutional desks.

04

Zero Automatic Censorship

We are a routing network, not a moderation tool. Institutional action is supported while staunchly protecting freedom of expression.
